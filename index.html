core.siyad.ch started as a napkin sketch that somehow became real. A small crew that likes building things — occasionally on purpose, sometimes by accident, always with strong opinions.

We believe good work happens when you stop overthinking the process and just start doing. We've been wrong about that at least four times. Still doing it anyway.

Based in Zürich, powered by caffeine, mildly confused about what our "brand" is. We think that's fine.

Our core methodology is vibecoding — we let the energy of the project guide the implementation. No overengineering, no 47-slide architecture diagrams. Just vibes, shipped fast.
